_____ is an authentic collection of real life art brought into the digital world. The first generation (Generation 0) consists of real life paintings from a mystery gallery that are professionally photographed and digitalized. Each piece of art has its own token and metadata which is hosted on IPFS (Interplanetary File System). While the metadata is not stored directly on the blockchain, an SHA-256 hash of each image is stored for the sake of provenance. Each piece of artwork can be owned by only one person at a time.

Generational Art

Every painting in this collection will have either one or two children. Every other generation will have two children, doubling the size of the next generation. This results in an increased amount of paintings with a decrease in value. The price of any painting can be calculated using the formula: 100/2x, where x is the generation of the painting (starting at 0). All paintings that are older than generation 0 have a parent, grandparent, etc. It's a family of art.
